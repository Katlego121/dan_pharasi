About Us

Learn More About Us

Our goal at Dan Pharasi is to provide children with a positive environment that will encourage them to explore the world around them and to reach their full potential.

  • We help children Academically.
  • There's various Sports in our school to help the children stay fit.
  • This school has a very good Culture and teach the children so they become better people and community members.

Education is one of the keys to success but knowledge is powerful. Not only do our teachers follow our South African curiculam but also teach life lessons so we can have educated teenagers. That can make a different to society and be mentally strong teenagers.

Why Choose Us?

We have dedicated teachers who recognise that education goes beyond the classroom. Within our warm family environment, we attempt to instill a set of core values enabling our pupils to participate in the school and the broader community as responsible, caring and productive human beings. In the words of Sir Arthur Helps, ‘Nothing succeeds like success’. All pupils are carefully guided towards experiencing successful outcomes leading to a healthy and safe learning environment.
